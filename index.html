Who is 'Kyary Pamyu Pamyu'?

Born as Kiriko Tamekura (竹村 桐子) in Nishitokyo, Tokyo Japan, she first debuted with the song "PONPONPON" in 2011. The music video exploded onto the scene and quickly reached the top ten on Japanes Oricon music charts. In 2012, she continued to release songs with similar success and finally debuted with her full Album "Moshi Moshi Harajuku". She is often hailed as the Queen of Harajuku Fashion and was given the title of "Kawaii Harajuku Ambassador" on August 30th, 2012 by the mayor of Shibuya.
